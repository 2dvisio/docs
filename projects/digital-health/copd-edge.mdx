# EDGE COPD

EDGE (Early Detection and Guidance for COPD Exacerbations) is a telehealth platform designed to support patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease through remote monitoring and early intervention.

## Overview

The platform helps patients and clinicians detect COPD exacerbations early, enabling timely intervention to prevent hospitalizations and improve quality of life.

## Key features

### Symptom monitoring
- Daily symptom questionnaires
- Breathlessness tracking
- Sputum color and volume reporting
- Activity level monitoring

### Early warning system
- Automated algorithms to detect symptom deterioration
- Alert generation for clinical teams
- Patient education on warning signs
- Self-management action plans

### Clinical workflow
- Clinician dashboard for patient monitoring
- Prioritized patient lists based on risk
- Secure messaging and teleconsultation
- Integration with electronic health records

## Clinical outcomes

EDGE COPD has demonstrated:
- Earlier detection of exacerbations
- Reduced hospital admissions
- Improved patient self-management capabilities
- High system usability and patient adherence

## Technical implementation

System components include:
- Mobile and web-based patient interfaces
- Clinical monitoring dashboard
- Rule-based alerting engine
- Secure data storage and transmission
- Integration with existing healthcare IT systems
- Analytics and reporting tools

## Research focus

The platform has been used to study:
- Predictive models for COPD exacerbations
- Patient engagement strategies
- Cost-effectiveness of remote monitoring
- Implementation in diverse healthcare settings
