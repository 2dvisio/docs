# SUMMIT-D

SUMMIT-D is a comprehensive digital health platform developed for clinical trials evaluating diabetes management interventions combining remote monitoring with clinical decision support.

## Overview

The platform integrates patient self-monitoring, telehealth consultations, and clinical decision support tools to optimize diabetes management and evaluate intervention effectiveness in research settings.

## Key features

### Patient engagement
- Blood glucose monitoring and tracking
- Medication logging
- Diet and activity tracking
- Educational resources

### Clinical decision support
- Treatment recommendation algorithms
- Glycemic pattern analysis
- Medication titration guidance
- Risk stratification tools

### Research capabilities
- Randomized controlled trial support
- Data collection and quality assurance
- Outcome measurement and reporting
- Protocol adherence monitoring

## Clinical trial design

SUMMIT-D supports:
- Multi-site clinical trials
- Randomization and blinding
- Intervention delivery and monitoring
- Standardized data collection
- Real-time data quality checks

## Technical implementation

System components include:
- Patient mobile and web applications
- Clinician decision support interface
- Research coordinator dashboard
- Secure data management infrastructure
- Integration with clinical trial databases
- Regulatory compliance (GCP, HIPAA)

## Research outcomes

The platform has enabled studies on:
- Effectiveness of telehealth interventions for diabetes
- Clinical decision support impact on glycemic control
- Patient engagement and adherence factors
- Implementation challenges in diverse populations
