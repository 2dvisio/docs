# SUPPORT-HF

SUPPORT-HF is a telehealth platform designed to support patients with heart failure through remote monitoring, self-management tools, and clinical oversight.

## Overview

The platform enables patients to track symptoms and vital signs while receiving personalized guidance and clinical support, aiming to reduce hospitalizations and improve quality of life.

## Key features

### Patient monitoring
- Daily weight tracking
- Blood pressure monitoring
- Symptom assessment (breathlessness, edema, fatigue)
- Medication adherence tracking

### Self-management support
- Educational content on heart failure management
- Personalized action plans
- Medication reminders
- Lifestyle guidance (diet, fluid intake, exercise)

### Clinical oversight
- Real-time data review by heart failure teams
- Automated alerts for concerning trends
- Secure communication channels
- Integration with clinical workflows

## Clinical outcomes

SUPPORT-HF has shown:
- Improved patient knowledge and self-care behaviors
- Early detection of decompensation
- Reduced emergency department visits
- Enhanced patient-provider communication

## Technical implementation

Platform architecture includes:
- Patient-facing mobile and web applications
- Clinical dashboard for healthcare teams
- Automated alerting and risk stratification
- Secure cloud-based data infrastructure
- Integration with home monitoring devices
- Analytics for population health management

## Research applications

The platform supports research on:
- Remote monitoring effectiveness in heart failure
- Patient engagement and behavior change
- Predictive analytics for hospitalization risk
- Implementation science in diverse care settings
