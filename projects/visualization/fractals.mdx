# Fractals and curves

Interactive web-based visualizations exploring fractals, space-filling curves, and geometric patterns.

## Available visualizations

### Fractal patterns

**Mandelbrot set**
- Classic fractal visualization
- Zoom and pan controls
- Color mapping based on iteration depth
- Exploration of self-similar structures

**Julia sets**
- Related to Mandelbrot set
- Parameter-driven variations
- Complex plane visualization
- Beautiful geometric patterns

### Space-filling curves

**Hilbert curve**
- Continuous fractal space-filling curve
- Demonstrates recursive construction
- Shows how 1D line can fill 2D space
- Multiple iteration levels

**Peano curve**
- Alternative space-filling approach
- Recursive subdivision pattern
- Mathematical elegance demonstration

### Other visualizations

- Geometric transformations
- Recursive patterns
- Algorithmic art
- Mathematical curiosities
