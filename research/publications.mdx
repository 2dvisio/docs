## Research themes

### Clinical effectiveness
Studies evaluating the impact of digital health interventions on:
- Clinical outcomes (glycemic control, exacerbation rates)
- Healthcare utilization (hospitalizations, ED visits)
- Patient-reported outcomes (quality of life, satisfaction)
- Cost-effectiveness and resource use

### Implementation science
Research on deploying digital health systems:
- Adoption barriers and facilitators
- Workflow integration
- Scalability and sustainability
- Healthcare provider perspectives
- Organizational factors

### Technology evaluation
Assessment of digital health platforms:
- Usability and user experience

- Data quality and completeness
- Patient engagement metrics

### Patient engagement
Studies on user interaction with digital health tools:
- Adherence patterns
- Engagement strategies
- Behavior change mechanisms
- Health literacy considerations
- Personalization approaches
